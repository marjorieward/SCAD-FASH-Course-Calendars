Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Half Scale Oaktag Slopers
    • 0.5 or 0.7 mm mechanical pencils
    • Erasers (if needed)
    • Gridded or alphanumeric pattern paper
    • Paper Scissors
    • ½ inch wide MATTE/SATIN FINISH scotch tape
    • Clear straight ruler with 1/8 inch markings
    • Pushpins or sewing pins
    • Fashion French curve/Varyform curve

During Class:

  • Skirt dart rotation and manipulation
  • Slash and Spread Sleeve modification
  • Drafting the Torso slopers
  • Drafting the Raglan sleeve slopers

After Class:

  • Practice drafting the Torso slopers and Raglan sleeve slopers in half scale.
  • Read pp.179-200, 333-336 (pp.211-232, 385-388) before Class Six.
